FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite as many team members as your plan allows. Upgrade your plan anytime to add more users.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

Go to Account Settings and select “Reset Password”. Follow the instructions sent to your email

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will remain active until the end of the billing period.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes. Our support team is available via email and live chat to help with any questions or technical issues.
